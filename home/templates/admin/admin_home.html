About VaxiUp

VaxiUp is an innovative system designed to streamline the vaccination process and ensure the health and safety of our community. Our platform allows administrators to manage vaccination centers, approve new centers, and keep track of vaccinated individuals.

Features

  • Manage and approve vaccination centers
  • View and manage user information
  • Track vaccinated individuals
  • Generate reports and analytics
  • Ensure data security and privacy
